Reads the three latest Hazeltree files written to SharePoint by the Power Automate flows. Works for anyone with access to the Treasury Team SharePoint site — no Claude or M365 connector needed.

Reading from SharePoint:
○ loan_board.csv ○ upcoming_payments.txt ○ upcoming_fees.txt
If files aren't updating, check that the Power Automate flows are turned on.

Load Files Manually

Use this when the automatic refresh isn't available. You'll need to locate three files from the daily Hazeltree emails and upload them here.

1 — Loan Board LD (CSV attachment)
Open the Loan Board LD email from GRV_PRD@hazeltree.com in your inbox. The email contains a CSV file attachment named something like Loan Board LD_YYYY-MM-DD.csv. Download that attachment and upload it here.
2 — Upcoming Payments (email body)
Find the Upcoming Payments email from Hazeltree. Forward it to yourself, then save it as a .msg or .eml file and upload it here. The payment data is in the email body — no attachment needed.
3 — Upcoming Fees (email body)
Same process as Upcoming Payments — find the Upcoming Fees email from Hazeltree, forward to yourself, save as .msg or .eml, and upload here.
